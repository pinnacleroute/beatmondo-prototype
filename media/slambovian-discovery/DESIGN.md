## Style Prompt

Archive Theatre blends beatmondo's dark cinematic luxury with the band's eccentric live-world energy. The footage stays dominant and authentic; typography behaves like a restrained editorial title treatment rather than a new band identity. Motion is deliberate at the opening, energetic through the performance, and quiet at the close.

## Colors

- `#120E0C` — warm near-black foundation
- `#F4EBDD` — ivory primary copy
- `#B2242A` — Gary Burke red brand accent
- `#C6A55D` — muted brass metadata and rules

## Typography

- `Fraunces` — expressive editorial display voice for the artist name
- `IBM Plex Mono` — compact metadata and beatmondo labels

## Motion

- Confident `expo.out` title reveal over live footage
- Restrained rule growth and metadata drift
- One quiet red-to-brass light pass at the close
- No continuous decorative loops or fabricated audio reactivity

## What NOT to Do

- Do not imitate or redraw the band's existing logo.
- Do not cover faces or instruments with large text.
- Do not use neon gradients, kaleidoscopic overlays, or novelty circus graphics.
- Do not imply that the excerpt or underlying music is cleared for licensing.
- Do not replace the authentic live footage with generated imagery.
